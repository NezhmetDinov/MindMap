Instructions

To begin, place your cursor under the company name listed in red bold lettering in the box on the left. Type any words on the next line that come to mind when you think about the company. There are no correct or incorrect answers. Be sure to hit the "Enter" key after every word.

When you are done entering the words, you may either wait for the timer to stop or click the "I'm done" button.
